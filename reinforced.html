Reinforced Water Activated Tape

Reinforced water activated tape is a high-performance carton sealing solution designed for applications where strength, security, and sustainability are critical.

Product Overview

Our reinforced water activated tape is fiberglass reinforced to provide superior tensile strength and permanent adhesion to corrugated cartons. Once applied, the tape bonds with the carton surface, creating a tamper-evident seal that cannot be removed without visible damage.

This makes it an ideal solution for export packaging, heavy cartons, and high-value shipments where conventional pressure-sensitive tapes are insufficient.
