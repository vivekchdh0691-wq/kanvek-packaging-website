Applications

Reinforced water activated tape is widely used across industries where secure, tamper-evident and durable carton sealing is required.

E-commerce Packaging

Ensures strong and tamper-evident sealing for e-commerce shipments, reducing transit damage and pilferage.

Export Packaging

Ideal for export cartons that require high strength, long transit durability and clear tamper evidence.

Warehousing & Logistics

Provides reliable sealing for cartons stored over long durations and transported through multiple handling stages.

Industrial Packaging

Suitable for heavy and overfilled cartons used in industrial and manufacturing environments.

Choose the Right Tape for Your Application

Our team can help you select the appropriate reinforced water activated tape based on carton weight and application requirements.
